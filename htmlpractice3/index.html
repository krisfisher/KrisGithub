Local Attractions of Lamoni, IA

Linden Street Coffee House

The Linden Street Coffee House has been a staple in Lamoni for many years reopening it doors in 2005.
it has been not only a staple for the local community but also a pupoluar hangout among Graceland students.
If your a fan of a good cup of joe then its best you stop in.

The Coliseum

The Coliseum is a local movie theatre that tends to stay up with the time despite its location and
small area of service. It not only shows current movies it also host multiple movie nights with throwback movies as well.
Be sure to stop in as you make your way through town and relax.

Graceland University

Graceland University is private liberal arts university in Lamoni, IA.
There mens basketball team won the national championship in 2018. The Lamoni campus offers over 50
academic programs and offers 22 varsity sports.

Livestock Auction

The Livestock Auction is a busy stop on thursday when the auctions are happening but the other days of
the week it is homes to amazing local eats. You can stop in for breakfast and walk away with some local amish
snakes. Just be sure not visit while on a diet.

Lamoni Community Center

Lamoni Community Center is an excellent place to host a gathering anytime.
It commonly hosts birthday parties for local children.
