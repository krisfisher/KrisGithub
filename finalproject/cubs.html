After a season where the Cubs fell short of repeating their history breaking World series win the Cubs
went into the off-season with focusing on improving their pitching staff but not over spending to get talent
the Cubs perfected this by not only getting the best starting pitcher in free agency in Yu Darvish they also
added a lot of veteran talent in bullpen by signing Brandon Morrow. With these two key acquisitions it puts
the Cubs into contention of having the best starting rotation in baseball but also gives them the flexibility
to go out after this season and maybe break the media by signing someone like Bryce Harper who has been rumored
to want to join the Cubs and play along side Kris Bryant who he played with in High School together. So needless
to say but Cubs fans should be happy about the talent on the roster and the possibility of the talent that will
be added in the possible future all while being able to compete and possible win another World Series this season.
